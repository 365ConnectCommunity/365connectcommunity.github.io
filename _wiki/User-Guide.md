Welcome to the 365Connect Community! This guide will help you get the most out of our platform.

## 1. Creating an Account
*   Click the **Login** button in the top right.
*   You can sign up using your **Email** or **Google Account**.
*   Once logged in, you will be redirected to your Profile.

## 2. Managing Your Profile
Navigate to **My Profile** to:
*   Update your **Profile Picture**.
*   Add your **LinkedIn** URL (helps networking!).
*   Write a short **Bio**.

## 3. Joining Events
1.  Go to the **Events** page.
2.  Click on any "Upcoming Event".
3.  Click **Register Now**.
4.  You will receive a confirmation, and the event will appear in **My Events**.

## 4. Earning Certificates
When you attend a workshop or complete a course:
1.  The instructor will issue a certificate to your email.
2.  Go to **My Certificates**.
3.  You can **View**, **Download**, or **Share** your certificate directly from there.
